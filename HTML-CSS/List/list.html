Dragon
A mythical creature that appears in the folklore of many cultures worldwide. Dragons are usually depicted as winged, scaly reptiles or serpents that breathe fire, have claws, and long tails.

A mythical bird that is known for being able to rise from its own ashes, representing immortality, renewal, and life after death.
